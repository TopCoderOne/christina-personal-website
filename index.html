what i do

photo shooting

Capturing the right photo is about more than just pressing the shutter. It’s about finding the perfect angle, lighting, and mood to tell your story visually. Whether you need portraits, product photography, or lifestyle images, the right shot can make your message stand out and connect with your audience instantly.

video shooting

When you’re creating a marketing video, the words you use have to work with the photos and videos you choose. That doesn’t just mean what the words are — it also means how you place them. Take a look at some tips for how to shoot text-friendly photos and video clips for marketing videos, and how to make the ones you already have work better in your video.

drone filming

Aerial footage brings a new perspective to your project that ground cameras can’t achieve. With drone filming, you can capture wide landscapes, dynamic movements, and unique angles that give your video or campaign a cinematic feel. Perfect for real estate, events, or promotional content, drone shots add scale and drama to your visuals.

location scouting

Finding the right place is just as important as the content you shoot. Location scouting helps set the tone and atmosphere for your photos or videos. Whether it’s a quiet street, a modern office, or a natural landscape, the perfect location can highlight your subject and elevate the final result.
